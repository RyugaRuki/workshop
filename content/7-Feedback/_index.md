## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The internship was conducted in a professional, collaborative, and innovation-driven environment. Throughout the program, I had the opportunity to work with modern cloud technologies, access well-organized learning resources, and practice in an environment that encouraged continuous learning and experimentation. The supportive atmosphere motivated me to explore new AWS services and strengthen my technical skills.

### 2. Support from Mentors and Program Team

One of the most valuable aspects of the internship was the guidance provided by the mentors. Rather than simply providing answers, they encouraged independent learning by directing interns to official AWS documentation, whitepapers, and best practices. The FCJ program team was also highly supportive, ensuring smooth communication, timely assistance, and reliable access to AWS Sandbox resources.

### 3. Relevance to My Academic Major

The internship aligned closely with my Information Technology studies at HUTECH University. Concepts learned in networking, operating systems, databases, and information security were reinforced through practical cloud projects involving AWS infrastructure, serverless applications, and cloud architecture design. This experience helped bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world implementation.

### 4. Learning and Skill Development

The internship provided excellent opportunities to improve both technical and professional skills.

From a technical perspective, I gained hands-on experience with core AWS services such as VPC, EC2, S3, RDS, IAM, Lambda, CloudFormation, and serverless architectures.

From a professional perspective, I improved my communication, teamwork, technical writing, and presentation skills through workshops, collaborative activities, and technical blog writing.

### 5. Company Culture and Team Collaboration

The internship promoted a collaborative and inclusive culture where every participant was encouraged to contribute ideas regardless of experience level. The friendly and supportive FCJ community made it easy to exchange knowledge, discuss technical challenges, and learn from one another throughout the program.

### 6. Internship Program and Resources

The internship offered a well-structured learning environment with access to AWS Sandbox accounts, official documentation, technical workshops, and community events. Flexible scheduling also allowed me to balance internship responsibilities with my university coursework while maintaining consistent learning progress.

## Additional Questions

### What did you find most satisfying during your internship?

The most rewarding experience was having the opportunity to build real cloud solutions on AWS while learning directly from experienced cloud engineers. In addition, becoming part of the FCJ community allowed me to expand my professional network and collaborate with students who share similar career goals.

### What could be improved for future interns?

I believe future internship programs could include more mock technical interviews, career development workshops, and one-on-one mentoring sessions to help interns better prepare for full-time employment.

### Would you recommend this internship to others?

Absolutely. I would highly recommend this internship to students interested in Cloud Computing, DevOps, and AWS. The program combines structured learning, practical experience, mentorship, and community support, making it an excellent starting point for a professional cloud career.

---

## Suggestions and Expectations

- Consider extending the internship by one or two additional months to allow interns to participate in larger projects and gain more real-world experience.
- Continue expanding technical workshops and hands-on cloud projects throughout the program.
- Create more opportunities for outstanding interns to contribute as community collaborators or mentors in future FCJ cohorts.
- Maintain and expand recruitment opportunities for high-performing interns after completing the program.

## Closing Remarks

I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the FCJ community, mentors, organizers, and everyone who supported me throughout this internship.

This experience has strengthened both my technical knowledge and professional mindset while inspiring me to continue pursuing a career in Cloud Computing and DevOps. I sincerely appreciate the opportunity and look forward to contributing to the community in the future.
